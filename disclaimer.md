# Disclaimer

## Non-Medical Product Notice

`femi` is not a medical application.

The app is intended only for personal tracking and organization of cycle-related information, symptoms, reminders, and notes.

`femi` does not provide:

- medical advice
- medical recommendations
- diagnosis
- treatment plans
- clinical interpretation of symptoms
- emergency support

Any information shown in the app is informational only and must not be treated as a substitute for professional medical care, diagnosis, or treatment.

Users should consult a qualified healthcare professional for any medical concerns, unusual symptoms, delayed periods, severe pain, reproductive health questions, or treatment decisions.

If a user may be experiencing a medical emergency, they must contact local emergency services or a licensed medical provider immediately.

## Data Handling Notice

`femi` is designed to be privacy-conscious.

The app does not sell personal data and does not share personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.

User data is processed only to operate the product's core functionality, such as:

- storing cycle history
- saving symptom and check-in records
- sending reminders requested by the user
- generating exports requested by the user

The app should not include ad trackers or third-party marketing analytics.

For technical operation, data may be processed within the product's own infrastructure and within the Telegram platform environment required to deliver the Mini App experience.

## Product Positioning Rule

All product copy, onboarding text, settings text, help content, and reminder messaging should remain consistent with this disclaimer.

The product must not imply that it:

- replaces a doctor
- evaluates health risk clinically
- recommends medication or treatment
- provides fertility or pregnancy guidance as medical advice

## Recommended Short Version

Use this short version in onboarding, settings, or footer surfaces:

`femi` is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. The app is intended only for personal tracking. We do not sell or share user personal data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.
