## Description

Status: open. Add a small set of high-value improvements without bloating the product. Features: stronger privacy options, optional local app lock or PIN, neutral notification text, delayed period and irregular cycle warnings, simple pattern detection across cycles, improved onboarding for non-fertility-focused users. Guardrails: no diagnosis, no medical claims, no fear-based messaging. See product-roadmap.md "Milestone 4: Quality-of-Life Improvements".
